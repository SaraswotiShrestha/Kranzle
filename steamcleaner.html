Steam Cleaners

Steam cleaners are the best tool for non-water soluble grime (grease, oil, body fats etc.) and sun-baked heavily soiled equipment. Kranzle's range offers up to 50% more efficiency than cold water high pressure cleaners. The heat melts away the grease and minimises drying times, saving you time and money.
